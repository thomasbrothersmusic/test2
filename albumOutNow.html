  • Realease Date: January 24, 2020
Recorded over four years, this album truly is a labour of love and a real family project. As the title suggests, "Together" pays homage to all that have played a part in the making of the album, including the boys sister, Esther Ruth, and their dad, David Thomas, who both lent their musical talents to the record. "For us, family is about being real, and so is our music. This album describes what we mean to each other, and the importance of unity. Like we kept saying when recording it, You can go fast alone... or you can go far together!"
